GHartisans

Category
Mobile App Design
Customer

Date

Location
Accra, Ghana

I design everything digital, interactive, and insight-driven to create products users love.

GHartisans is a mobile platform designed to seamlessly connect customers with skilled artisans trained and certified by the Youth Employment Agency (YEA). The app features two separate interfaces—GHartisans for customers to browse, book, and review trusted professionals, and GHartisan+ for artisans to onboard, showcase their services, and manage job requests. In addition to the mobile experience, I also designed a comprehensive admin dashboard that allows YEA administrators to monitor activity, manage users, track performance, and ensure smooth operations across the platform. Built for reliability and accessibility, GHartisans streamlines the service process, promotes employment, ensures quality workmanship, and provides a safe, organized way for people across Ghana to access essential artisan services.

  • Mobile UIUX design
  • Dashboard Design
  • Prototyping
  • User Research
  • Presentation to Stakeholders at YEA Head Office
  • Collaboration with Developers
This admin dashboard was designed to give YEA full control and visibility over the GHartisans ecosystem. It provides a streamlined interface for managing artisans and customers, tracking service requests, monitoring activity, and ensuring smooth operations across the platform. The design focuses on clarity, efficiency, and ease of use, enabling administrators to make informed decisions quickly and confidently.
